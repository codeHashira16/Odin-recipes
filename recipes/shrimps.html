Garlic Butter Shrimp

Shrimp is a delicious and versatile ingredient that can be used in a variety of dishes. Here's a recipe for garlic butter shrimp that is simple to make and bursting with flavor.This garlic butter shrimp recipe is a crowd-pleaser and perfect for a quick and easy weeknight dinner. You can also serve it as an appetizer or as part of a seafood platter. Give it a try, and let your taste buds enjoy the delicious flavors of this dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and peppe
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

How to make it

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add the butter and olive oil.
  2. Once the butter has melted and the mixture is hot, add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes.
  3. Cook for about 30 seconds, or until the garlic is fragrant
  4. Add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side or until they are pink and opaque.
  6. Add the chopped parsley and lemon juice to the skillet and stir to combine.
  7. Serve the garlic butter shrimp hot with your favorite sides.
